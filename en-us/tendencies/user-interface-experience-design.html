In today’s busy world, providing your customers with a user-friendly digital experience can make or break your business. How customers interact with your digital presence will determine the quality of your relationship.

Research has shown that whether your customers like or dislike your website or mobile app is 94% design related. This points out that UI/UX Design is the crucial factor in determining the success or failure of a website or mobile app.

Today, Mordwerg is excited to give you the information you need to make sure you are getting the most out of your digital presence through the creativity of UI/UX Design.

What is UI/UX Design?

All of your favorite websites and mobile apps have been carefully designed to make your experience intuitive and aesthetically pleasing. Two types of designers are utilized to create great UI/UX experiences.

User Interface (UI) Designer

User Interface Designers take the layout from the User Experience Designers and turn them into the beautiful designs you see within your favorite apps and websites. They add color, animations, and all graphical elements to create a high fidelity end product referred to as your digital presence.

User Experience (UX) Designer

User Experience Designers work closely with businesses to understand their goals and to deeply understand who their customers are. Using their knowledge and understanding they will then create a website or app layout which will use content strategy and strategically placed calls to action to direct customers down specific routes to achieve your goals.

Why is UI/UX Design important?

Statistics show that frustrated customers will drastically hinder growth and sales. The combination of well thought out UX Design and beautifully implemented UI Design will save costs, increase revenue and better retain customers.

User Interface Design (UI)

An application’s user interface plays an important role in the overall experience it offers its users. Knowledgeable user interface design companies know that the relationship between UI and UX is a highly complimentary one in which both form and function work together to deliver an integrated, seamless experience. By working from this master document, we can ensure that all the UI work we put forth is consistent, intuitive, functional, and delivers a strong return on your investment through increased usability.
